## The Producer

Clarendelle is where one of Bordeaux's most rarefied names meets everyday drinking. Domaine Clarence Dillon is the family behind Château Haut-Brion, a Premier Grand Cru Classé of 1855 and one of the five most prestigious wine estates in the world. Clarendelle was created as a way to offer the Haut-Brion team's expertise and philosophy in an approachable, ready-to-drink format.

The rosé is the newest addition to the Clarendelle portfolio and represents something genuinely unusual: a pink wine made under the direction of the same winemaking team responsible for one of Bordeaux's greatest reds. It is not a side project or afterthought. The same eye for balance, the same pursuit of elegance over weight, and the same insistence on freshness that defines Haut-Brion runs through this wine at a completely different scale.

## Viticulture

Fruit is drawn from carefully selected vineyards across Bordeaux. The soils are typical of the region: gravel, clay-limestone and sandy deposits providing excellent drainage. The selection process ensures only parcels that meet the house standard of elegance and balance make it into the final blend.

## Winemaking

Grapes are harvested at optimal maturity and undergo gentle pressing with minimal skin contact to extract pale, delicate juice. Fermentation takes place at controlled temperatures in stainless steel to preserve freshness, fruit purity and aromatic vibrancy. The wine is designed to be enjoyed young, drinking beautifully on release.

## Tasting Notes

Pale, iridescent salmon-pink hue. The nose is powerful and expressive, with wonderful hints of tropical fruit led by lychee and pomegranate, alongside ripe strawberry and raspberry aromas. On the palate it is solid and well-balanced, with a smooth structure and tangy fruit that provides liveliness. The finish is fresh and clean, with a gentle persistence.
